Curriculum Vitae

I am an economist at the Banca d'Italia and hold a PhD in economics from the Stockholm School of Economics. Prior to my PhD studies, I obtained a BA (Hons) in economics from the University of Cambridge. My reseach, falling in the intersection of macroeconomics and finance, is concerned with the implications of different information structures on aggregate outcomes.
