Curriculum Vitae

I am an economist at Banca d'Italia and hold a PhD in economics from the Stockholm School of Economics. Prior to my PhD studies, I obtained a BA (Hons) in economics from the University of Cambridge. My research spans a wide range of topics: informational role of the price system, asymmetric information in financial markets, asset pricing and policy evaluation.

Research Interests
